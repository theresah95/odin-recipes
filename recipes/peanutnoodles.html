Peanut Noodles

Description

This is a recipe for peanut noodles by the website Drive Me Hungry.

It takes only 15 mins and it is always delicious!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 large english cucumber
  • 2 medium carrots
  • Thai peanut sauce
  • handful of cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil your dried noodles according to the directions. Drain and rinse with cold water afterwards.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the Thai Peanut Suace
  3. In a large bowl, toss together the cooked noodles, peanut sauce, cucumber, carrots, cilantro. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
